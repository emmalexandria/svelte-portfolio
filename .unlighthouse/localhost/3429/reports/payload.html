About me

I'm a passionate web developer and designer. I have a particular passion for web typgraphy. Outside of work, I enjoy developing command-line tools and applications. I have experience in a wide variety of technologies, ranging from Rust to Python. In my free time, I enjoy argumentative writing, listening to albums, and making whatever pops into my mind.

Technologies

I work with a wide variety of technologies. My favourite frontend combination is Svelte with TailwindCSS. I find most backends are served well enough by a PocketBase instance. However, I also have considerable experience with Next.js and Hugo. I'm currently learning Vue and Nuxt.js. I typically design mockups in Figma, and logos and other assets in Illustrator.
